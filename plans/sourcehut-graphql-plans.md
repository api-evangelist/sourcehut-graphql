# Sourcehut GraphQL Plans

Sourcehut (sr.ht) operates on a pay-what-you-can subscription model. All tiers
provide identical access to all services, including the full GraphQL API. There
is no functional difference between plans — the tiers exist to allow users to
contribute what they can afford.

## Subscription Tiers

| Tier                | Monthly Price (EUR) | Monthly Price (USD) |
|---------------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Amateur Hackers     | €4/month           | $4/month           |
| Typical Hackers     | €8/month           | $8/month           |
| Professional Hackers| €12/month          | $12/month          |

Taxes are calculated at checkout and are not included in the listed prices.

## Service Payment Requirements

Some services require an active paid subscription while others are optional
during the public alpha phase:

**Payment Required:**
- builds.sr.ht (CI/CD)
- chat.sr.ht

**Payment Optional:**
- git.sr.ht
- hg.sr.ht
- todo.sr.ht
- lists.sr.ht
- Project Hub (hub.sr.ht)
- SourceHut Pages (pages.sr.ht)
- paste.sr.ht

## Financial Aid

Sourcehut offers financial aid for users who cannot afford a subscription.
Contributors who are not hosting their own projects may use Sourcehut for free.

Source: https://sourcehut.org/pricing/
