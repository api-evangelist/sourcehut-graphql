dispatch.sr.ht is a SourceHut service which provides integrations between SourceHut and third-party services like GitHub and GitLab, and it is scheduled to be deprecated and shut down. Here’s the plan: 2022-08-01 : Creation of new dispatch tasks is disabled. 2022-09-01 : Dispatch users will be emailed with information about migration.
