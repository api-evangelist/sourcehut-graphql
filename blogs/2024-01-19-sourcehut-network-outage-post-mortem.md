It’s been a busy couple of weeks here at SourceHut. At the time of writing, we have restored SourceHut to full service following an unprecedented 170 hour outage, and while we still have numerous kinks to sort out following an unscheduled emergency migration of all services across an ocean, all services are now fully operational. Allow me to open this post-mortem by extending my deepest apologies to the SourceHut community for this interruption in service.
