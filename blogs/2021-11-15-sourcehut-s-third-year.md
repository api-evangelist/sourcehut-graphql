Three years ago today, I announced that SourceHut would be making its alpha open to the general public after two years of development in private. Since then, in addition to moving across the Atlantic ocean, I have overseen the development of a service, and a business, which has grown to exceed all of my expectations. Today, SourceHut is home to 25,000 users, 5,000 projects, 42,000 git repos and 5,000 Mercurial repos.
