About one month ago, we began a private beta for chat.sr.ht , the next flagship sourcehut product. Starting today, this service is now available to all paid sourcehut users. chat.sr.ht is a hosted IRC bouncer service, which maintains a persistent IRC connection for you and extends IRC with useful features like offline messaging, log persistence, and various other improvements.
